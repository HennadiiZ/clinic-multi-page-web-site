Get Relief from Knee, Back, Shoulder, Hip, and Ankle Pain

Knee, back, shoulder, hip, and ankle pain are some of the most common medical concerns among adults in Miami. However, many of them avoid seeing a sports medicine specialist until their nerves and joints suffer extensive damage. Our joint pain experts at the Top Doc Clinics in Miami offer a broad range of non-invasive medical treatments that can eliminate inflammation and pain from any joint in your body. Avoid costly knee, shoulder, and hip replacement surgeries by talking to our clinicians and seeing which regenerative treatment will work best for you.

Hormone Therapy

Hormone therapy is an effective way to treat both chronic pain and symptoms of menopause. Our clinic uses hormones and neurosteroids to fight uncomfortable menopausal symptoms and to treat pain.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

No one needs a reminder that oxygen is vital to life, but did you know that oxygen does more than help you breathe? It is also essential for injury recovery and wound healing.

Cryotherapy

Cryotherapy is a cold therapy method that relies on below-freezing temperatures to push your body into survival mode by compressing peripheral arteries and increasing metabolism.
