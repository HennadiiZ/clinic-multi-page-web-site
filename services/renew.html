Laser Facials

Laser facials or laser resurfacing is a safe and effective cosmetic procedure to clean and rejuvenate your skin. At Top Doc Clinics in Miami, FL, we offer professional laser facial treatments that effectively remove scars, wrinkles, blemishes, and hyperpigmentation.

Corrective Aesthetic Dental Procedures

Issues with your teeth can not only cause pain and infections; they can also severely damage your self-esteem. From teeth whitening to dental implants, our cosmetic dental procedures will get you smiling with confidence again.

Maxillofacial

Perfect oral health includes making sure your teeth are not only healthy but also function correctly together with the surrounding structures. Maxillofacial surgery or oral surgery can effectively treat many injuries, diseases, and defects in the jaw, face, neck, and soft tissues of the mouth.

Cryotherapy

Cryotherapy is a cold therapy method that relies on below-freezing temperatures to push your body into survival mode by compressing peripheral arteries and increasing metabolism.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

No one needs a reminder that oxygen is vital to life, but did you know that oxygen does more than help you breathe? It is also essential for injury recovery and wound healing.

Red Light Therapy
