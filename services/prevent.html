Men’s & Women’s
health Evaluations

Get on top of your health with annual men’s and women’s health evaluations from Top Doc Clinics in Miami, Florida. Find out what appointments you should be making and schedule personalized treatments today!

Supervised Diet & Nutrition Programs

Our clinic’s medically supervised weight loss and nutrition programs help you lose weight safely and effectively. After our team assesses your overall health, we will discuss your nutritional goals and develop a specialized plan that fits your lifestyle.

Vaccinations

The best way to protect children from preventable diseases is with vaccinations. However, vaccinations are not just for children; they also prevent infections in adolescents and adults.

Men’s Health

It’s no secret that preventative care is one of the most important things. That’s why our men’s health services include regular prostate and testicular cancer screenings using state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment.

Women’s Health

Individualized care is the cornerstone of our women’s health services. We cover everything from gynecology and breast health to fertility and prenatal care. We design our treatment options around your specific needs.

STD Checks

Sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) or infections (STIs) are a major health concern. There are more than 20 kinds of STIs, and they can cause multiple diseases. STDs can occur due to exposure to viruses, bacteria, parasites, or yeast.

Laboratories

At Top Doc Clinics, we offer an extensive array of laboratory tests. You can be confident that any test or referral order you submit at our CLIA-accredited laboratories will receive top priority treatment from qualified professionals.

Genetics Screening

Your DNA is what makes you unique. That’s why examining it with genetics screening for changes known as mutations or variants is vital. Your test results can help you better understand inherited disorders.

Diagnostics Assessments

Our Medical doctors use diagnostics assessment to confirm or rule out the presence of suspected illness in patients. These diagnostic tests are important for detecting diseases in asymptomatic patients, assessing disease progression.

Functional Medicine

Functional Medicine is a 21st-century medical field that takes a science-based approach to patients’ healthcare needs. It shifts away from traditional disease-based approaches to treatment and focuses more on holistic, patient-centered care.
